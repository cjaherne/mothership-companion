# Another Bug Hunt - Themes & Tone

> Developer: Fill in horror beats, atmosphere notes, and what to emphasize for the voice agent.

## Setting

- **Samsa VI**: Jungle planet, raging tropical storm, radio blackout
- **Greta Base**: Abandoned terraforming colony, ransacked, no power
- **Carcinids**: Crab-like aliens, insect/spider elements, chest-bursting reproduction (Aliens-inspired)

## Tone

- Atmospheric dread over jump scares
- Environmental storytelling: what happened here?
- One monster = director's chair. Use the Carcinid sparingly.
- Announcing danger before visibility: rhythmic thumping before the beast appears

## Horror Beats to Emphasize

1. **Approach**: Storm, silence, abandoned facility
2. **Discovery**: Ransacked rooms, signs of violent assault
3. **The Sound**: Rhythmic thumping—they know something is wrong before they see it
4. **Vents**: Everyone understands vents in horror. Use them.
5. **Power On**: Lights, jukebox, waking the thing—sometimes you choose the moment

## Content Warnings

Arachnophobia, body horror, violence, claustrophobia.
